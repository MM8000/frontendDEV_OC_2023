For those who draw, sketch, paint, or create visual art, the iPad Procreate app has been one such game changer. Designed to be used with Apple’s tablet and stylus tools, Procreate gives artists all the benefits of working digitally while recreating the familiar experience of pens, pencils, and paint on paper." Game development from the perspective of a hobbyist. "You don't need to spend the time, effort, and money on large programs designed for professional game designers if all you're trying to accomplish is to have a minimum viable product at first. For JohnDav, procreate was the answer. A simple tool with some powerful features like the ability to layer images, tons of awesome creative brushes, and the ability to create color pallets from images or live on the spot through the camera of the iPad. The best feature of Procreate though is that the iPad is such a game changer when it comes to portability. Being able to work on game assets on the go whenever creativity sparks in one's brain has since been a real benefit."
